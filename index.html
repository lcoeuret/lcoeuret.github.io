About Me

From September 2024, I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the Dipartimento di Matematica Tullio Levi-Civita of the University of Padova supervised by Fabio Ancona, Laura Caravenna, Elio Marconi and Roberta Bianchini. I defended my PhD on the 12th of July 2024. It was supervised by Jean-François Coulombel and Grégory Faye at the Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse.

Main research interest

Numerical analysis of systems of conservation laws

  • Stability of discrete shock profiles for systems of conservation laws.
  • Stability of finite difference schemes for the transport equation in 1D on the whole and the half-line.
