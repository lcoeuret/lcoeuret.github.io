From September 2025, I am a Maître de conférences in Applied Mathematics (Section CNU 26) at the Université de Lorraine in Nancy. I am a member of the Institut Élie Cartan de Lorraine (IECL) and I am teaching at Informatics department of the IUT Nancy-Charlemagne.

Between September 2024 and September 2025, I was a Postdoctoral researcher at the Dipartimento di Matematica Tullio Levi-Civita of the University of Padova. I was supervised by Fabio Ancona, Laura Caravenna, Elio Marconi and Roberta Bianchini in the project PRIN 2022 PNRR Heterogeneity on the Road: Modeling, Analysis, Control.

I defended my PhD "Stability of discrete shock profiles for systems of conservation laws" on the 12th of July 2024. It was supervised by Jean-François Coulombel and Grégory Faye at the Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse.
